About the Business

Halal House is one of Northridge’s newest eateries. It’s become a hit spot for college students on a budget looking for a variety of flavors. The menu includes foods from many different parts of the world. We combined the original Greek concept with sauces that come from India and Pakistan. Afghan-style rice made various spices and food from all over the Middle East. You can even order US-inspired Crispy chicken sandwiches and House burgers.
